L. Ron Hubbard

About

L. Ron Hubbard was born on March 13, 1911. He was an author who later founded the Church of Scientology. He was the Commodore of the Sea Organization, the fleet of Scientology boats. His wife Mary Sue Hubbard was indited for espionage against the United States Government for her role in the Snow White Program. He died in January 24, 1986. The Church of Scientology is still around and has attracted many high name celeberties such as Tom Cruise and John Travolta.
